BRAD PITT


Brad Pitt is a celebrated American actor and film producer, born on December 18, 1963, in Shawnee, Oklahoma. Renowned for his charisma and versatility, he rose to fame with standout performances in films like Legends of the Fall, Fight Club, Troy, and Mr. & Mrs. Smith. Over the years, Pitt has proven his range through both intense dramatic roles and lighthearted comedies. His performance in Once Upon a Time in Hollywood earned him an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor. Beyond acting, he co-founded the production company Plan B Entertainment, which has produced critically acclaimed films like 12 Years a Slave and The Departed. Pitt is also known for his humanitarian work, particularly in rebuilding homes after Hurricane Katrina through the Make It Right Foundation.
